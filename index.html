Swipe

Saving money is now as easy as a swipe. Swipe left to discard, swipe up to send to a friend or swipe right to keep and use an offer. Once in your Swoup Wallet, we organize your saved offers by retailer, product category and expiration.

Offers you find on Swoup can be used when you go shopping at participating retailers either in-store or online, whichever you prefer. Simply give your retailer loyalty card or phone number at checkout and your dsicount are automatically applied.
